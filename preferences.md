# Website Design Preferences

## Things I Like

1. **Floating/flying info cards** — small pill-shaped cards (like "500+ Students", "30+ Countries") that float around the photo with a gentle bobbing animation (seen in v4 Organic and v8 Playful)
2. **Bodoni Moda font** — the dramatic, high-contrast serif display font used in v7 Dark Editorial for the hero heading
3. **Large hero font size** — the oversized heading treatment from v7 (clamp 3.5rem to 9rem), fills the viewport with bold presence
4. **Overall style of v5 Retro 70s** — warm cream background (#FFF8F0), warm oranges/burnt sienna color palette, "Righteous" display font, circular photo frame with dashed orbit rings, floating decorative circles, rounded bubbly shapes, warm nostalgic vibe
